Lampshade Party

  • High Concept: Play as a fragile lamp at a frat party that must fend off incoming drinks without toppling over.
  • Player Experience Goals: Humor
  • Game play: Use the up arrow key to jump and twice to double jump. The left/right keys perform a dash and the down key is a free-fall. Try not to have your lampshade hit the ground for as long as possible.
  • Prototype goal: Controlling awkwardly shaped physics objects in a limited space.
  • Story: You're a precious antique lamp in the middle of a wild frat party. Drinks are flying everywhere and there's nowhere to hide. You have to try you best not to completely topple over on your side to survive.
  • Strategies: Let drinks hit you to spin you to a favorable position.
